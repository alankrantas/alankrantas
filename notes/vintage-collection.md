## Film Cameras

- AE = automatic exposure mode (P = program, A = aperture priority, S = shutter priority, M = manual/uncoupled meter)
- SLR = single lens reflex
- TLR = twin lens reflex
- RF = rangefinder
- VF = viewfinder
- P&S = point & shoot
- ?E/?G = ? element(s) in ? group(s)
- eq. = (35mm focal length) equivalent
